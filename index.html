NetworkWhat is Network?

This is a simple definition of a network; A network consists of two or more computers that are linked in order to share resources(such as printers and Cds), echange files, or allow electronics communications. There are two very common types of networks include:LAN and WAN.

PAN(Personal Area Network)

A personal area network is a computer network for interconnecting electronic devices with an individual person's workpace. A PAN provides data transmission among such as computers, smartphones, tablets and personal digital assistants.

Learn more

LAN(Local Area Network)

A local area network(LAN) is a collection of devices connected together in one physical location, such as building, office, or home.

Learn more

MAN(Metropolitan Area Network)

A metropolitan area network is a computer network that interconnects users with computer resources in a geographic region of the size of the metropolitan area.
